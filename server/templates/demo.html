Multi-Party Computation Demonstration

This page showcases a technical demonstration of Multi-Party Computation (MPC) used in the Innovation Center's Internet of Things (IoT) project. The demo was created by Carlton Shepherd and Pradip Mainali.

It uses utility classes for typography and spacing to space content out within the larger container.
